The project involves studying, as well as play-testing, different multiplayer games in order to gain a first hand perspective to hypothesize a core relationship between player and an interaction. Production of a multiplayer game, where users can interact with one another, as well as, the objects that surround them was vital; especially in acquiring the analysis to the aforementioned relationship.
