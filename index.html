About

Abdullah is a Cybersecurity Master's degree holder at St. Cloud State University and Honorary Computer Systems graduate of Heriot-Watt University whose goal is to take part in the creation and protection of memorable, interactive, entertaining and narrative-driven games that people of all backgrounds can enjoy.

He is a passionate and hardworking professional with a talent in optimizing, refactoring, visualizing concepts and writing and maintaining documentation. Abdullah has an extensive background in C#, C++, optimization, refactoring, engine/library development, utility development, network programming and game design.
