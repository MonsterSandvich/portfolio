Behind The Door

Behind The Door is a story about a skeptical detective, John, who is investigating the abandoned's house massacre where group of teenagers were killed. On early night Detective John arriving to the abandoned house to find out what has happened there but suddenly he realized that he is not alone.
Implemented UI integration for Behind The Door with different movement setups for the Android release of the game.
Developed for the Steam release, giving vital feedback and testing for bugs.

Release Date August 16, 2017
Category Game Design, Quality Assurance,
English Localization
Employer Dio Alias Games
