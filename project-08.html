Don't Starve Together - Workshop

Made a Crash Bandicoot mod for Don't Starve Together (DST), a game by Klei Entertainment, using Valve's Steam Workshop. The project involved creating sketches, creating art pieces with the proportional anatomy of DST, rigging the sprites and animating them, managing a team remotely, coding with Lua and HTML.
The mod currently sits as one of the top favorited character mods with over 200,000 downloads.
It was nominated during the Indie Game Devfest as a Dubai Game Expo Finalist.
