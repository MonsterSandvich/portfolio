Call of Duty®: Warzone

Call of Duty®: Warzone is a free-to-play battle royale video game. Released for PlayStation 4, Xbox One, and Microsoft Windows. A version for the PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X/S and mobile is in development.
Worked with mutiple studios accross different continents including Beenox, High Moon Studios, Sledgehammer Games, Toys for Bob and Activision Shanghai.
The title was nominated for Best Multiplayer and Best Ongoing at The Game Awards 2020.
